ATTENTION

Please read the below information to receive your package in a timely manner.
Canada Post Parcel Tracker

Some customers miss the Canada Post delivery attempt notice.
When they realize their packages have not arrived, it has already been returned to our company.

To avoid this situation, please check the Canada Post website with your tracking number often.

If you forget to check the tracking status and if Canada Post keeps it for 30 days (expedited) and 5 days (Xpress), your package will be returned to us. It will cost you!

To Talk to a Canada Post Representative

  1. Phone 1-866-607-6301 and talk to a Canada Post customer representative.
  2. Give the representative your tracking number.
  3. Ask for the address, phone number, and hours of operation of the local postal unit your package is being helt at.
  4. You can schedule a redelivery or pick it up in person.
    For pick up, your photo ID is required.

If you forget to check the tracking status and if Canada Post keeps it for 30 days (expedited) and 5 days (Xpress), your package will be returned to us.
It will cost you!

The Canada Post service will hold your package for up to 30 days (expedited) and 5 days (Xpress), after that they will return the package to us and we will then contact you to decide if you want to cancel your order or have it resent.

The following charges then apply:
Option 01 : Resending

$30 resending fee will apply.
We do not make any money on this service, as Canada Post charges us $18.00 to send your package back to us and it costs $12.00 to resend it to you.
(We consume labour and packaging material costs at our expense.)

Option 02 : Cancelling the Order
$20 cancellation fee will apply to those wishing to cancel for whatever reason.
This fee only covers the return shipping cost of $18.00 and the credit card transaction fees, we do not make any money on.
(We consume labour and packaging material costs at our expense.)

*Note : Our customer service center has no more information or ability than what the customer can see and do on the Canada Post tracking site.
